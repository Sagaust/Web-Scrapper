Ballon d'Or winner and 1st runner up including other star players no go make di first ever Winter World Cup

The controversy behind Qatar hosting di World Cup no bin fit stop anything as football fans go witness di first ever Winter FIFA World Cup.

As di mata take be, plenty players wey pipu for like see for no go feature.

Some of the biggest names wey no go feature include,

Sadio Mané

(Senegal/Bayern Munich) - Injury

Karim Benzema

(France/Real Madrid) - Injury

Paul Pogba

(France/Juventus) - Injury

Victor Osimhen

(Nigeria/Napoli) - Did not qualify

Gianluigi Donnarumma

(Italy/PSG) - Did not qualify

Mohammed Salah

(Egypt/Liverpool) - Did not qualify

Riyadh Mahrez

(Algeria/Manchester City) - Did not qualify

Sergio Ramos

(Spain/PSG) - Not called up

David de Gea

(Spain/Manchester United) - Not called up

Reece James

(England/Chelsea) - Injury

Erling Haaland

(Norway/Manchester City) - Did not qualify
