The Nigerian Women's Football team go play friendlies against Japan Women's football Team for Kobe, Japan

The Super Falcons of Nigeria wey bin recently cari 4th posi for inside Women's AFCON 2022 wey shele for Morocco go play against Japan wey be 11th for world ranking.

The coaching staff and some players don already arrive Japan since Monday. The match itself go happen for Misaki Park Stadium, Kobe on the 6th of October 2022.

Super Falcons wey don lose their last 2 friendlies with aggregate of 6-1 against USA go dey look to get something out of this friendly match against Japan.
