Ndaba Sibanda

Biography

Ndaba Sibanda is a Bulawayo-born poet, novelist, and nonfiction writer. A prolific and critically-acclaimed writer, he is the author of The Gushungo Way, Sleeping Rivers, Love Oâ€™clock, The Dead Must Be Sobbing, Football of Fools, Cutting-edge Cache, Of the Saliva and the Tongue, When Inspiration Sings In Silence, The Way Forward, Poetry Pharmacy, Sometimes Seasons Come With Unseasonal Harvests, As If They Minded, and The Dangers Of Child Marriages: Billions Of Dollars Lost In Earnings And Human Capital. Ndaba has been nominated for National Arts Merit Awards, The Pushcart Prize and Best of the Net.
