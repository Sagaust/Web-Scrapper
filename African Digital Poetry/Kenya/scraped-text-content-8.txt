Mukoma wa Ngugi

Biography

Mukoma Wa Ngugi was born in Evanston, Illinois in 1971, and grew up in Kenya. He holds a BA in English and Political Science from Albright College, an MA in Creative Writing from Boston University, and a PhD in English from University of Wisconsin, Madison. He is the author of the poetry collections, Hurling Words at Consciousness (2006) and Logotherapy (2016), as well as novels and critical books on African literature and politics. A prolific writer and academic, Mukoma Wa Ngugi was shortlisted for the Caine Prize for African Writing in 2009 and the Penguin Prize for African Writing in 2010. He is the co-founder of the Mabati-Cornell Kiswahili Prize for African Literature.
