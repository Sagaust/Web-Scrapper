Michael Weeder

Biography

Michael Weeder was born in Capetown, South Africa. He earned his BA (Hons) and an MA in History from the University of the Western Cape (UWC). He is the author of Lockdown, Love and Lament (2020, African Lives) and The Promise of Memory (2021, African Perspectives Publishing). He is also the Dean of St George’s Cathedral in Cape Town.
