Nadine Botha

Biography

Nadine Botha was born in 1979 in Pietermaritzburg, South Africa. Poet, journalist and curator, she is the author of two poetry collections: Compared to Not Eating Tuna and Chocolate (2013) and Ants Moving The House Millimetres (2005). She went to Rhode University for her BA in Theory of Art and in 2015, she earned her MA in Design Curating and Writing from Design Academy Eindhoven. As a research designer, Botha’s work explores how political, scientific and cultural systems shape the designs in our in material reality. She lives in Rotterdam, Netherlands.
