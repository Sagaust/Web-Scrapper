Ingrid de Kok

Biography

Ingrid de Kok was born 1951 in Johannesburg, South Africa. She is most well known for her collections Familiar Ground, Transfer and Terrestrial Things, where she explores the possibilities of shaping the future by dealing with the past and considering the present.. She went to university of University of Witwatersrand for her undergrad in English and Political Science. She also received a Bachelor Honors with honors from the University of Cape Town and a masters at Queenâ€™s University. She continued on to become an Associate Professor at the at the Centre for Extra-Mural Studies, University of Cape Town in South Africa.
