Liesl Jobson

Biography

Liesl Jobson was born in Durban, South Africa. She is most well known for her collections 100 Papers, a book of prose poems and flash fiction, and View From The Escalator. She went to the University of the Witwatersrand for her undergrad in creative writing. She also received her masters in music and creative writing. She is the winner of the Inglis House Poetry Contest (2004), the POWA Womenâ€™s Writing Poetry Prize (2005), the Ernst van Heerden Creative Writing Award (2006) and the Faye Goldie Award (2007). She was also the national editor of Poetry International Web, South Africa.
