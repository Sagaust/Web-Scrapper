Stephen Gray

Biography

Stephen Gray was born 1941 in Cape Town, South Africa. Poet, novelist and playwright, he explored homosexuality, apartheid, as well as racial and sexual issues in South Africa. He went to the University of Cape Town for his undergrad in English. He also received his MA from Cambridge University in England and his MFA from the University of Iowa. He became an English lecturer at the Rand Afrikaans University (later the University of Johannesburg) in 1969, where he was awarded D. Litt and d. Phil. by Rand Afrikaans University, Johannesburg in 1978. In 2007, Gray received a South African Literary Award for Lifetime Achievement. He died in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2020.
