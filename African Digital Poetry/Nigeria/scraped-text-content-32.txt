Mary-Alice Daniel

Biography

Mary-Alice Daniel was born 1986 in Maiduguri, Borno State, Nigeria and raised in England and Nashville. Her chapbook, Blood for the Blood God, was published by The New Generation African Poets Series in 2017. Her first book of poetry, Mass for Shut-Ins, is the winner of the 2022 Yale Younger Poets Prize and is forthcoming from Yale University Press in April 2023. She received her BA from Yale University in 2008 and her MFA from University of Michigan in 2013. Also, in 2021, she received her doctorate degree in English Literature from University of Southern California.
