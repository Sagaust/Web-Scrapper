Habeebatu X

Biography

Habeebatu X was born in 1997 in Ilorin, Nigeria. She studied Medicine at the Bayero University Kano and has an MBBS degree. She is currently a House Officer in the University of Ilorin Teaching Hospital, Kwara State of Nigeria.
