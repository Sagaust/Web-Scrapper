Tolu Ogunlesi

Biography

Tolu Ogunlesi was born in Edinburgh, Scotland in 1982, and grew up in Nigeria. He attended University of Ibadan, where he received a Bachelor of Pharmacy in 2004. He also has an MA in Creative Writing from University of East Anglia. A writer and award-winning journalist, Ogunlesi is the author of the poetry collection, Listen to the Geckos Singing from a Balcony (2004). He was awarded the Dorothy Sargent Rosenberg poetry prize in 2006, and the Nordic Africa Institute Guest Writer Fellowship in 2008. In 2016, he was appointed Special Assistant on Digital/New Media to Nigerian president, Muhammadu Buhari.
