Ishmael Fifi Annobil

Biography

Ishmael Fiifi Annobil was born in Accra, Ghana in 1958. A musician, journalist, filmmaker, poet, and critic, he is the author of Seven Horn Elegy (Totem, 1998) and Ethiop (Totem); his work has been anthologized in Another English: Anglophone Poems from Around the World.
