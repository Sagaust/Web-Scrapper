Victoria Adukwei Bulley

Alternate names: Adukwei

Biography

Victoria Bulley was born in 1991in London. She is the author of the chapbook collection, Girl B (Akashic), selected as part of the New-Generation African Poets series and winner of an Eric Gregory Award. Poet, writer, and filmmaker, her work was shortlisted for the African Poetry Prize in 2016. She went to University of Kent for her undergraduate studies in English & Drama (2013). She also received her masters in Postcolonial Studies from SOAS University of London in 2015. Her debut poetry collection, Quiet, is set to be released in 2023. Bulley is a Creative Writing doctoral student at Royal Holloway, University of London.
